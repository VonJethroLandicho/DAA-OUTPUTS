Subset Sum Group Selector

Finds students matching score, study hours, and stress level using backtracking.

Description

This script uses an iterative backtracking approach to find a group of students who meet specified constraints such as minimum/maximum scores, study hours, and maximum stress level.

How It Works

The program performs these steps:

  1. Loads Dataset: Reads from Students_Grading_Dataset.csv
  2. Filters Students: Based on predefined criteria like score, study hours, and stress level
  3. Uses Backtracking: To select the best possible group of students
  4. Displays Results: Shows student info in table + bar graph showing Total Score, Study Hours, and Stress Level

Execution Details

  • Dataset Used: Students_Grading_Dataset.csv
  • Score Range: 70 – 90
  • Study Hours per Week: 10 – 20
  • Max Stress Level: 5
  • Number of Students Required: 5
  • Algorithm: Iterative Backtracking

Sample Criteria Used


Minimum Total Score: 70
Maximum Total Score: 90
Minimum Study Hours: 10
Maximum Study Hours: 20
Stress Level: 5
Number of Students: 5

The selected students are displayed using both a bar chart and a table. Each student meets all the required criteria.
